About

Building applications through years of design.

My career began in graphic design and web design, where I spent more than 14 years creating print and digital experiences for businesses, public campaigns, large organizations, and high-profile events. That work taught me how thoughtful design improves communication, builds trust, and helps people accomplish their goals.

As my focus shifted toward technology, I moved into web and application development, applying that same user-focused process to enterprise software. Today, I build applications that balance usability, maintainability, and secure development practices while continuing to expand my knowledge of networking and application security.

Rather than leaving my design background behind, I use it to strengthen the software I build. Understanding both user experience and technical implementation helps me create applications that are practical, intuitive, and solve problems.
