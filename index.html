Engineering Perspective

From design thinking to secure software development.

My professional career began in digital and web design, where I spent over 14 years creating solutions for businesses, large organizations, public campaigns, and high-profile events. Working on projects viewed by thousands taught me that successful technology must communicate clearly, solve real problems, and earn the trust of its users. Today, I bring that same philosophy to software development. I build enterprise web applications that are secure, maintainable, and intuitive—combining full-stack development with a user-centered mindset to create software that works as well for the people using it as it does for the teams maintaining it. My background in design isn't separate from my work as a developer—it's one of my greatest strengths. It enables me to bridge the gap between business goals, user needs, and sound software engineering practices.

My design background remains one of my greatest strengths—not because I create visuals, but because I understand the people who use the software.
